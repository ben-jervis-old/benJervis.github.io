What is civitasCRM

civitasCRM is a customer relationship management tool designed for small to medium sized organisations and non for profits. It is a streamlined tool with a core set of features to maximise functionality while maintaining usability.

Project Aims

With CivitasCRM we aim to provide St Marks with a system that will allow it to effectively manage its contact with the congregation. A web application will allow for a secure, portable, reliable, and multiplatform system that will be able to achieve the core components that are required, which are an effective attendance system, multiplatform and portability, mass contact, grouping, and streamlined.

Features

We further aim to deliver a complete system that includes all of the following features:

  • Add, update, and delete users and their contact details
  • Mark attendance of users at various services and events
  • Analyse attendance data and provide alerts for excessive absences
  • Organise users into a diverse and varied system of groups
  • Mass contact, sent to individual users, groups, group leaders, or all users
  • Generate and run custom reports
  • Store and display photos of all users

Design Approach

CivitasCRM has been designed with a User-Centred Design in mind and this is due to the fact that this approach tries to optimise the product around how the users can, want, or need to use the product rather than forcing the users to change their behaviour to accommodate the product.

Client

St Mark's Church

St Marks's is a small Anglican church located on Crown street in west wollongong.

Need

The church would like a Customer relationship management tool to properly service all members of their congregation.

This tool would allow them to keep track of members attendance, keep member details and organise rosters for services and group meetings.

Issue

Although there exists on the market already tools that can solve all these needs, unfortunatly they are too expensive for small organisations such as St Marks or they come with bloated software with too many features.

St Marks church would like a system that is not only accessible to their technically literate admin staff but also volunteers helping the church.

Solution

With these issues in mind civitasCRM aims to be a lightweight, intuitive and effective CRM tool.

civitasCRM will aim to be an accessible tool for all levels of technical knowledge while still being powerful enough in functionality to properly service St Mark's Church.
